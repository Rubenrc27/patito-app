# Design System: Rubber Duck Surveys

## Colors
- **Primary Deep Navy:** `#0F172A` (Main brand color, text, dark backgrounds)
- **Secondary Yellow:** `#FACC15` (Accent, duck color, highlights)
- **Tertiary Blue:** `#3B82F6` (Interaction, links, secondary buttons)
- **Background Light:** `#F8FAFC` (Surface color, light mode)
- **Surface White:** `#FFFFFF`
- **Error Red:** `#BA1A1A`
- **Neutral Gray:** `#64748B`
- **Border Gray:** `#E2E8F0`

## Typography
- **Headlines:** Montserrat (Bold/SemiBold) or Inter
- **Body:** Inter (Regular)
- **Data:** Inter (Medium/Tabular numbers)

## Corner Style
- **Buttons/Cards:** 12px (Rounded)
- **Inputs:** 8px

## Depth
- **Subtle:** 0 4px 20px rgba(15, 23, 42, 0.05)
- **Layered:** Glossy icons, soft shadows on cards.

## Mood
- **Modern & Clean:** Professional survey tool.
- **Playful:** "Quack" energy, duck icons, bright yellow accents.
- **Responsive:** Adapts from mobile to desktop.
