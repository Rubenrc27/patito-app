# Expanded Prompt: Professional Mobile Showcase

## Style Block
- **Palette:** 
  - BG: Soft mesh gradients (`#F8FAFC` to `#E2E8F0`)
  - Device: Dark Deep Navy (`#0F172A`) with subtle metallic highlights.
  - Accent: `#FACC15` (Duck Yellow)
- **Typography:** Inter
- **Mood:** Premium, Professional, "SaaS Landing Page" aesthetic.

## Rhythm Declaration
`intro-FEATURE-FEATURE-ANALYTICS-cta`

## Global Rules
- **The Hero:** A centralized CSS-based mobile device mockup (iPhone 15 Pro style).
- **Parallax Background:** Floating soft-blurred spheres and grid lines that move slowly.
- **Interactions:** A circular "touch indicator" (`#3B82F6`) that appears to simulate taps.

## Per-Scene Beats

### Scene 1: The Device Reveal (0-6s)
- **Concept:** The mobile device rotates into the center from a perspective view. The screen glows and reveals the Splash screen.
- **Mood:** Cinematic, high-end reveal.
- **Animation:** 
  - Device: Rotates 3D from `rotateY(45deg)` to `0deg`.
  - Splash: Duck icon PUNCHES onto the screen inside the mockup.
- **Transition:** Hard cut (screen remains fixed).

### Scene 2: Infinite Feed (6-13s)
- **Concept:** Simulating a user scrolling through the "Estanque".
- **Animation:**
  - Touch Indicator: Appears at bottom, swipes UP.
  - Content: Cards scroll vertically within the mobile frame.
  - Feature Highlight: One card is "tapped", scales slightly, and a ripple appears.
- **Transition:** Vertical Push (simulating navigation).

### Scene 3: Swift Creation (13-20s)
- **Concept:** Showing how easy it is to create a survey on mobile.
- **Animation:**
  - Mockup UI: Shows a form.
  - "Typing": Text fields fill with "Nueva Encuesta" (typing effect).
  - Tap: Indicator hits the "CREAR" button.
- **Transition:** Blur resolve.

### Scene 4: Insights in your Pocket (20-26s)
- **Concept:** Real-time analytics view.
- **Animation:**
  - Chart: A line graph or bar chart DRAWS itself inside the mobile screen.
  - Stat: Total participants number COUNTS UP rapidly.
- **Transition:** Shader Cinematic Zoom into the screen.

### Scene 5: Final CTA (26-30s)
- **Concept:** The mobile screen fades to the Duck Yellow brand color, then pulls back to show App Store badges.
- **Animation:**
  - Device: Scales down and drifts to the left.
  - Text: "Disponible ahora" SLIDES in from right.
  - Badges: FADE IN below the text.
